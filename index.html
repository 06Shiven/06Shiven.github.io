About Me

I’m a 19-year-old engineering student specialising in backend systems, robotics algorithms, and applied AI. I served as Team Captain of Brampton Robotics Team 11400X and as a programmer for Team 1104X. I developed odometry-based navigation systems to enable high-precision autonomous routines during competitions. I also designed and tuned custom PID controllers for drive control, arm mechanisms, and trajectory following, optimising performance across a range of dynamic environments.

My leadership involved architecting modular subsystems, writing efficient autonomous logic, and mentoring team members through iterative design, system debugging, and match-day strategy. This contributed to four VEX Worlds Finals qualifications and consistent top-tier provincial performance.

I also interned at MR. STUDIOS, where I worked on backend automation tools, built CRM-integrated real estate landing pages, and deployed asynchronous scripts to streamline internal marketing workflows.

Current: Building reinforcement learning agents, embedded AI platforms, and scalable backend systems. I’m driven by a passion for intelligent automation, control theory, and human-AI interaction.
